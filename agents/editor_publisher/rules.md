# Editor/Publisher Rules

## Decision Authority

The Editor/Publisher makes the final accept/revise/reject decision after reading all referee opinions and integrity reports.

## Decision Process

1. Treat integrity failure as a hard publication gate.
2. Compare Researchers as competitors using the same shared dataset and the same resource constraints.
3. Treat serious plagiarism or fabricated citations as a hard publication gate.
4. Treat severe selective reporting or unsupported headline claims as a hard publication gate.
5. Consider all referee recommendations, scores, originality concerns, citation concerns, and revision requests.
6. Prefer manuscripts that combine credible evidence, clear presentation, and original/creative framing.
7. Accept at most one winning manuscript in this minimal community.
8. If no submission merits acceptance after integrity and referee review, record that no manuscript was published.
9. Publish the accepted manuscript as a text-only PDF and archive the decision record.
