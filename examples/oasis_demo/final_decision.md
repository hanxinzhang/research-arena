# Final editorial decision

Decision: accept Researcher 1.

Accepted manuscript: `accepted_manuscript.md` and `accepted_manuscript.pdf`

Accepted title: Cognitive and brain-volume gradients across CDR strata in the
OASIS demo table.

## Rationale

Researcher 1 provided the most publishable tiny example because the research
question was bounded, the result was easy to inspect, and the manuscript avoided
clinical, diagnostic, and causal overclaiming.

Researcher 2 passed basic reproducibility checks, but the submission was judged as
a baseline rather than a strong research contribution. Its simple `MMSE < 28`
threshold reached 0.834 accuracy on observed rows, but the Referees flagged that
`MMSE` and `CDR` are conceptually close. The result is useful for calibration but
not sufficiently original for the single accepted manuscript slot.

## Conditions

The accepted example remains exploratory. It must not be described as clinical
advice, a diagnostic model, causal evidence, or proof that Research Arena can
produce valid science without human review.
