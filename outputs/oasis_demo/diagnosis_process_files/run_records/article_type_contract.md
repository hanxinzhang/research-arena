# Article Type Contract

Run id: `oasis_demo`

Selected article type: compact demo article.

The run tests whether the Research Arena workflow can produce a bounded,
reviewable article package from a local OASIS cross-sectional table. The
article is not a clinical validation study and must not claim external
deployment readiness, causal effects, or novelty beyond the compact demo.

Acceptance bar:

- two independent Researchers complete Phase 0 pilots before analysis;
- each Researcher proposes a distinct question;
- revision_00 contains executable analysis, results, manuscript, figures,
  tables, and a presentation checklist;
- revision_01 addresses evidence-linked referee or integrity issues;
- the Editor may accept at most one bounded article package;
- final claims must be exploratory and demo-scoped.
