# Agent Independence Plan

Researcher 1 and Researcher 2 use separate proposal-gate folders,
revision folders, and role workspaces. They may use shared framework
templates and the common OASIS dataset, but their questions, analyses,
manuscripts, and revision responses are authored as separate submissions.

Referees and the Integrity Checker write separate role artifacts under
`agents/<role>/workspace/oasis_demo/`. Deterministic clerks are evidence
tables only and do not make scientific acceptance decisions.
