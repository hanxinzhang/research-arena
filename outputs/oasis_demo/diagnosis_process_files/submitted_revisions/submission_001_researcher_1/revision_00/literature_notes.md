# Literature Notes

This compact demo uses the OASIS cross-sectional table as a workflow
test case rather than a literature-backed clinical claim. The endpoint
is Clinical Dementia Rating, which is commonly used as a dementia
severity marker. Predictors such as age, MMSE, and morphometric
quantities are biologically and clinically plausible but require
external validation before any translational interpretation.

The manuscript therefore frames the analysis as an internal
discrimination exercise and cites the dataset documentation, the
Clinical Dementia Rating construct, and standard cross-validation
practice.
