# Research Arena Brand Assets

Primary logo direction:

- `research_arena_logo_evidence_gain.svg`

The logo uses a minimalist sans-serif `Delta E` mark beside a stacked wordmark:
`Research`, `Arena`, and `EVIDENCE GAIN`. The concept is evidence gain through
adversarial review, revision, and evolutionary selection.

Font policy:

- Use open-source fonts only.
- Prefer the bundled Inter font family in `assets/fonts/inter/`.
- Acceptable open-source fallbacks are Source Sans 3, Noto Sans, and Liberation
  Sans.

Keep the logo simple enough to work in article PDF headers, title blocks,
grayscale review copies, and small exported figures.
